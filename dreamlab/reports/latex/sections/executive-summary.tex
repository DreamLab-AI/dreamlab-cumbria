DreamLab AI Consulting represents a revolutionary convergence of cutting-edge artificial intelligence training facilities with sustainable holiday accommodation in the heart of Wales. Our unique business model combines:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{AI Training Centre}: State-of-the-art laboratory equipped with high-performance computing infrastructure for hands-on AI/ML training
    \item \textbf{Eco-Luxury Accommodation}: Sustainable holiday lets featuring solar pergola, EV charging, and off-grid capabilities
    \item \textbf{Dual Revenue Streams}: Corporate training packages (weekdays) and holiday lettings (weekends/holidays)
\end{itemize}
