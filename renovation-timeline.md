### Critical Path Items
1. **Trifold doors** - Must complete before kitchen (weather-tight)
2. **Solar pergolas** - Required before battery connection
3. **Lab partition** - Essential before equipment installation
4. **Planning approvals** - Gates all major works

### Risk Mitigation
- **Weather delays**: Indoor work scheduled for winter
- **Contractor availability**: Book 3-6 months ahead
- **Material shortages**: Order long-lead items early
- **Budget overruns**: 15% contingency included
- **Family disruption**: Phase works to maintain livability
