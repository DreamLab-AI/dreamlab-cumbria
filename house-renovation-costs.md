### Cost Optimization Strategies

1. **Bundle Work**: Combine electrical work for sauna, battery, and solar
2. **Winter Scheduling**: Book outdoor work for spring/summer 2026
3. **Material Sourcing**: Use local suppliers to reduce transport costs
4. **VAT Efficiency**: Install battery with solar for 0% VAT
5. **R&D Tax Credits**: Claim on innovative energy systems

### Planning Considerations

1. **Listed Building**: Check any heritage restrictions
2. **National Park**: Solar pergolas within PD rights (<10m² each)
3. **Building Control**: Required for trifold doors and major alterations
4. **Party Wall**: Check requirements for chimney work
