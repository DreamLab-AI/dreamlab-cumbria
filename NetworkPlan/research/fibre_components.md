### Notes

- **All prices include VAT and reflect Black Friday discounts where available (FS.com UK).**
- **OM4 is recommended for short runs (up to 300m, 10GBASE-SR). OS2 is only needed for long runs or 10GBASE-LR.**
- **Ubiquiti UFiber modules are available and compatible with Ubiquiti switches. FS.com modules are also Ubiquiti-compatible if coded at purchase.**
- **For a single 10G link, an 8-fibre cable is sufficient (two fibres used, six spares).**
