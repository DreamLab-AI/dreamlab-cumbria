# DreamLab AI Consulting - Integrated Executive Summary
## Comprehensive Business Plan with Property Development

### Project Overview

DreamLab AI Consulting represents a pioneering venture combining executive AI training with luxury holiday accommodation in the Lake District. The project involves converting Fairfield House in Eskdale Green into a dual-use facility, strategically positioned 30 minutes from Sellafield's nuclear complex.

**Total Investment Required: £330,458 - £399,234**

- **Phase 3 (Months 10-15):** External works - solar pergolas (6kW), garden clearance, shed
- **Phase 4 (Months 16-18):** Leisure items - outdoor sauna, trampoline pit, custom shelving

- Business setup and marketing
