DreamLab AI Consulting has identified a significant market opportunity in establishing an executive training lab in Eskdale Green, Lake District. The venture capitalizes on two converging trends: the nuclear industry's urgent need for executive training (87,000 workforce growing to 211,500 by 2050) and the Lake District's premium holiday let market.
