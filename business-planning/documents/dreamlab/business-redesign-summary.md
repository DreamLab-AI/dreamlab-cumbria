# DreamLab AI Ltd Business Redesign Summary
## From Nuclear Engineering to Creative Technology Leadership

### Executive Overview

This comprehensive business redesign repositions DreamLab AI Ltd from a nuclear engineering-focused training facility to a pioneering creative technology and engineering simulation center. The new positioning maintains nuclear sector services (20% of revenue) while expanding into high-growth markets including virtual production (50%), engineering visualization (20%), and emerging technologies (10%).

### Key Changes Implemented

#### 1. Market Positioning
- **Before**: 70% nuclear industry focus, targeting Sellafield ecosystem
- **After**: 50% creative technology, 20% engineering simulation, 20% nuclear, 10% other
- **Rationale**: Creative tech markets growing at 45% CAGR vs 12% for traditional engineering

#### 2. Service Portfolio Transformation

**New Core Offerings:**
1. **Virtual Production Masterclass** (£4,995, 5 days)
   - LED volume operations
   - In-camera VFX workflows
   - Real-time rendering

2. **Gaussian Splatting & Neural Rendering** (£2,995, 3 days)
   - Photogrammetry pipelines
   - 3D reconstruction
   - Neural radiance fields

3. **Telepresence & XR Development** (£2,495, 3 days)
   - Holographic communication
   - Remote collaboration
   - Enterprise XR

4. **Engineering Visualization** (£3,495, 4 days)
   - CAE/CFD/FEA visualization
   - Digital twin development
   - VR for engineering

5. **AI for Creative Industries** (£2,495-3,195, 2-4 days)
   - Generative AI workflows
   - ComfyUI mastery
   - ML for content creation

#### 3. Facility Enhancements

**Creative Technology Infrastructure:**
- 6m × 2.5m LED volume for virtual production
- 48-camera gaussian splatting capture array
- Motion capture studio (12m × 8m)
- Telepresence lab with holographic displays
- Drone testing facility (indoor/outdoor)

**Maintained/Enhanced:**

- Sustainable energy systems (expanded to 18kW solar)
- Residential accommodation with tech amenities
- Collaboration spaces with digital tools

**Old Positioning**: "Transform nuclear industry leadership through immersive AI and VR training"
