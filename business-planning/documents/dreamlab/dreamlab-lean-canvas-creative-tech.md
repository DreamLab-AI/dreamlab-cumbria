# DreamLab AI Ltd - Lean Canvas (Creative Tech + Engineering Simulation Focus)

## 1. Problem
- **Creative industries lack practical AI/ML training** focused on production workflows
- **Engineering simulation disconnected from creative visualization** (gaussian splatting, telepresence)
- **No residential training combining virtual production + engineering simulation**
- **Skills gap in emerging tech** (drone autonomy, XR, real-time rendering)
