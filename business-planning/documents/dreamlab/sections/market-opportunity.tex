\subsection{Wales Technology Sector Growth}

\begin{keypoint}
Wales is experiencing significant growth in its technology sector:
\begin{itemize}
    \item 12\% annual growth in tech businesses (2020-2024)
    \item \pounds{2.1} billion contribution to Welsh economy
    \item 40,000+ employed in tech roles
    \item Strong government support through Tech Wales initiative
\end{itemize}
\end{keypoint}

\subsection{Holiday Let Market in Wales}

The Welsh holiday let market provides strong secondary revenue potential:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Average occupancy rate: 72\% (rural properties with unique features)
    \item Average nightly rate: \pounds{150-250} for luxury eco-properties
    \item Growing demand for ``workation'' properties with high-speed internet
    \item 35\% increase in eco-tourism bookings (2022-2024)
\end{itemize}
