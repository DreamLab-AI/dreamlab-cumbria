\chapter{Overview and Principles}

\section{Purpose of Marketing Collateral}

Marketing collateral serves as the tangible representation of the DreamLab brand, enabling consistent communication across all client touchpoints. Each piece should:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Reinforce our position as the integrated creative technology leader
    \item Demonstrate our ``mesh fluency'' across AI, immersive, and creative
    \item Build trust through professional presentation
    \item Drive specific actions (enquiry, meeting, partnership)
    \item Reflect our values of accessibility and innovation
\end{itemize}

\section{Core Design Principles}

\begin{tcolorbox}[dreambox, title=The Five Pillars of DreamLab Design]
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Clarity First}: Complex technology made simple and accessible
    \item \textbf{Visual Hierarchy}: Guide the eye to key messages and CTAs
    \item \textbf{Brand Consistency}: Every element reinforces our identity
    \item \textbf{White Space}: Let designs breathe for premium feel
    \item \textbf{Purpose-Driven}: Every piece has a clear objective
\end{enumerate}
\end{tcolorbox}

\section{Presentation Templates}

\subsection{PowerPoint/Keynote Structure}

\begin{tcolorbox}[dreambox, title=Standard Deck Structure]
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Title Slide}: Company name, presentation title, date
    \item \textbf{Agenda}: Clear navigation for the deck
    \item \textbf{Challenge}: Problem we're solving
    \item \textbf{Solution}: Our approach and capabilities
    \item \textbf{Proof}: Case studies and results
    \item \textbf{Process}: How we work
    \item \textbf{Team}: Key people involved
    \item \textbf{Commercials}: Pricing and timelines
    \item \textbf{Next Steps}: Clear CTA
    \item \textbf{Contact}: All contact details
\end{enumerate}
\end{tcolorbox}

\subsection{Slide Design Guidelines}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Maximum 6 bullet points per slide
    \item Minimum 24pt font for body text
    \item 30\% text, 70\% visuals ratio
    \item Consistent header placement
    \item Footer with page numbers and confidentiality notice
\end{itemize}

\chapter{Content Guidelines}

\section{Writing Style}

\subsection{Voice and Tone}

\begin{tcolorbox}[dreambox, title=DreamLab Writing Principles]
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Accessible}: Technical concepts explained simply
    \item \textbf{Confident}: We know our stuff, but stay humble
    \item \textbf{Action-Oriented}: Focus on outcomes and benefits
    \item \textbf{Inclusive}: Avoid jargon, welcome all readers
    \item \textbf{Optimistic}: Positive about possibilities
\end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}

\subsection{Grammar and Style Rules}

\begin{itemize}
    \item UK English spelling throughout
    \item Oxford comma usage is optional but be consistent
    \item Sentence case for headlines (not Title Case)
    \item Active voice preferred
    \item Present tense for capabilities
    \item Numbers: Spell out one to nine, use digits for 10+
    \item Percentages: Always use digits (5\% not five percent)
\end{itemize}

\section{Typography Quick Guide}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Headlines}: Montserrat Bold, -20 tracking
    \item \textbf{Subheads}: Montserrat Medium, 0 tracking
    \item \textbf{Body}: Inter Regular, +10 tracking, 1.5 line height
    \item \textbf{Captions}: Inter Light, 90\% size
    \item \textbf{CTAs}: Montserrat Bold, all caps, +50 tracking
\end{itemize}

\section{Common Mistakes to Avoid}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Using RGB colours in print materials
    \item Stretching or distorting the logo
    \item Mixing fonts within same hierarchy level
    \item Overcrowding designs - embrace white space
    \item Forgetting mobile optimisation
    \item Ignoring accessibility contrast ratios
    \item Using outdated statistics or case studies
    \item Creating materials without clear CTAs
\end{enumerate}
