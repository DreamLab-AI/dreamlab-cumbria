\begin{executivesummary}
This document presents the comprehensive project timeline for the Business Excellence Initiative, detailing all major phases, milestones, and deliverables across a 24-month implementation period. The timeline integrates product development, market entry, and scaling activities with clear dependencies and resource allocations.
\end{executivesummary}

\section{Timeline Overview}

The project spans 24 months, divided into four major phases:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Foundation Phase (Months 1-6)
    \item Development Phase (Months 7-12)
    \item Market Entry Phase (Months 13-18)
    \item Scaling Phase (Months 19-24)
\end{enumerate}

\section{Critical Path Analysis}

The critical path through the project includes:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Business planning and legal setup (3 months)
    \item Core team recruitment (2 months)
    \item Product design and architecture (3 months)
    \item Platform development (4 months)
    \item Testing and pilot programme (2 months)
    \item Marketing campaign launch (2 months)
    \item Customer acquisition ramp-up (6 months)
    \item Series A fundraising (3 months)
\end{enumerate}

Total critical path duration: 24 months with 1 month buffer.

\section{Risk Mitigation Timeline}

\begin{riskbox}
Key timeline risks and mitigation strategies:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Development delays}: 2-month buffer built into development phase
    \item \textbf{Recruitment challenges}: Early start on hiring, use of contractors
    \item \textbf{Market entry timing}: Flexible launch window between months 12-14
    \item \textbf{Fundraising delays}: Alternative funding sources identified
\end{itemize}
\end{riskbox}
